SPILMAN THOMAS & BATTLE.

The team and I built a unique legal website for the corporate law firm – Spilman Thomas & Battle. They hired a marketing firm in Michigan to design the website and hired ASP, which included me at the time, to build the website and provide local website support. The team built the website with a unique menu, navigation, and searches. The website cleverly displays services, people, firm information, careers, and locations. The resources section has a search and contains articles, press releases, e-newsletters, events, webinars, news, Spilman alerts, and videos. I worked on moving over the content from the old site to the new site. I collaborated with the lead designer at ASP to recreate interior pages that needed additional functions.
